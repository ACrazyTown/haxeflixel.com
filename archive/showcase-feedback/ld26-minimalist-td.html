Gama11
7 months 2 weeks ago

@Beeblerox Oh, yeah, the particles... I made a hacky little change to my flixel install for those, something like (in FlxParticle.update()):

 

alpha -= 0.01;

if (alpha <= 0) kill();

 

So since that took care of killing them, I probably didn't set a lifetime. This actually also gave me the idea to do the FlxParticle improvements. Since you're probably compiling with the 1.09 version, these particle effects are bugged and also look crappy I guess.

 

Recycling enemies and particles is definitely a good idea. Shouldn't be too hard, but I didn't really pay attention to it since I figured it shouldn't really be an issue on desktop for the LD version - and so far, no-one has really complained about lag.

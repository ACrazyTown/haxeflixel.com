I really like the concept and especially the artstyle - very innovative.  Reminded me of SHIFT. 

 

That being said, the controls are a little awkward. Not only because Z is used and Z and Y are switched on QWERTY-keyboards, which are used in Germany for example (always keep that in mind when making games using either one of those keys and allow both), but also because of the delay in controls and the fact that you cannot control the mid-air-movement yourself when jumping.  Jumping shouldn't automatically make you move horizontally.

 

The delay when switching sides makes it very difficult to react to events that would otherwise be easy to handle - I think you might just want to get rid of the animation for that or speed it up a lot, being frozen for that time is no fun. Also, I was a bit confused about the shield mechanics - they didn't really seem to save me from hits in the second level.

 

I'm kinda thinking I've heard the music elsewhere before... 

 

A post-compo-version would probably be a good idea.

 

As for the "no-colissions-bug" after x = 600 - Flixel normally only does colissions in game dimenion's area to save performance. So, if you have a scrolling tilemap that is larger than that area,  you have to adjust that area in which colissions are done by changing FlxG.worldBounds. Which is, admittedly, not very intuitive.

Hello pierrebousquie, a game a month would be an awsome way to get your hands dirty in game design. That is awesome you got to meet Nicolas his work is very inspiring 

 

Since you know java I imagine you might be picking up Haxe rather quickly your already seem to know the basics with bubble clash. I read your impression "should not have updated" , nme and even haxe 3 are under heavy development for the better. The last couple of nme releases broke things but we caught up pretty quickly on github. You will find the haxelib commands very helpful, just toggle back the nme or flixel version you were working from, also let us know about bugs or help us out either here or on the github issue queue; 

 

haxelib set flixel 1.08 /// 1.07 etc haxelib dev flixel /path/to/your/git/clone/flixel/src haxelib dev // to toggle back to a haxelib version

 

Enjoy learning Haxe, and cramming one game a month in, here or the nme forums are a great places for feedback ^_^

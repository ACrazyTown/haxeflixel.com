stage size, mouse behavior

Garfunkel, 8 months 3 weeks ago

Just some small things, but for anybody who runs into it, for instance when making a gui layer on top of flxgame instance:

The stage height grows bigger than the height you put into flxgame. That's because of flixel's soundtray that is positioned in negative coordinates. You can simply remove it or whatever and the stage will remain what you put into flxgame.

 

Also mousebehavior in haxeflixel gets a bit funky when you initialize flxgame with usesystemcursor=true. It sort of updates half of the flixel mouse things. Position is ignored but pressed sort of works. So if you do use the systemcursor, do not depend on flixel's mouse, or make sure to call FlxG.mouse.show(), possibly with a transparent 1x1 pixel bitmap or so.

 

And if your components grab the focus, like input fields or so, you'll need to give it back to flixel, like so: Lib.current.stage.focus = null;//get the focus off of any components, so flixel keys respond again

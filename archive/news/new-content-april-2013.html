New Content - April 2013

crazysam, 8 months 1 week ago

Hey guys,

 

This month was a little slower in terms of updates to the documentation and the engine, but there were some interesting developments:

 

Haxe3:

The haxe3 branch has been tested by a few people and seems to be in order. There might be some features that haven't been tested, but the transition to haxe3 should be seamless when it arrives.

 

Multithreading:

The dev branch of HaxeFlixel contains threading optimizations that seem to give a strong performance boost for devices with multiple cores. This feature is still highly experimental and some things seem to break with it (nape was acting strange on my Android device). But it's only a matter of time until the kinks get ironed out. Follow the discussion on the forums!
